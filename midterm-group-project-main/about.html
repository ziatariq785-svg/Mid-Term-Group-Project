About Us

  • Every day, thousands of people enter the gates of City Care Hospital (CCH) with someone close to them in need of medical attention.
  • Some come for routine check-ups, while others arrive in critical condition requiring immediate care.
  • Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by a team of dedicated healthcare professionals committed to providing exceptional care.
  • We offer a comprehensive range of medical services, from primary care to specialized treatments, ensuring that our patients receive the best possible care tailored to their individual needs.
  • At CCH, we prioritize patient safety and comfort, creating a healing environment that fosters recovery and well-being.
  • We are also deeply committed to community health, engaging in outreach programs and health education initiatives to promote wellness and prevent illness.

Vision

  • To be a leading healthcare institution recognized for excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community health promotion.
  • To create a healing environment that fosters recovery, well-being, and trust among patients and their families.
  • To continuously advance medical knowledge and technology to provide the highest quality care.
  • To promote health equity and accessibility for all members of our community.
  • To cultivate a culture of compassion, respect, and collaboration among our healthcare professionals.
  • To lead initiatives that improve public health and wellness in the communities we serve.

Mission

  • To provide exceptional, patient-centered healthcare services that meet the diverse needs of our community.
  • To deliver compassionate care with respect, dignity, and empathy for every patient and their family.
  • To maintain the highest standards of medical excellence through continuous education, research, and innovation.
  • To ensure a safe and healing environment for patients, staff, and visitors.
  • To foster a collaborative and supportive workplace that empowers our healthcare professionals to deliver their best.
  • To engage with and contribute to the health and well-being of the communities we serve through outreach and education programs.
